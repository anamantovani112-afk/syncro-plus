This is the exact same episode used in the teacher-led lesson — this version is just built for you to work through alone.

1. Open the video above in a new tab.
2. Watch normally until you reach the timestamps below — then pause.
3. Listen to the line a couple of times, write the expression you hear, and guess its meaning before checking.
4. Keep watching after each pause. At the end, answer the comprehension questions in your own words.

Section 1

Catch the Expression

Pause at each timestamp below
⏸ ~09:35

Reviewing a new intern's file, a senior doctor says he only just barely qualified to get into the program. Listen for a 4-word idiom meaning "to just barely succeed or qualify."

"barely made the cut" — to just barely qualify or succeed at meeting a required standard.
⏸ ~09:40

Explaining a strategy for keeping a group of interns in order, a doctor says punishing one intern will make the rest start behaving the same way. Listen for a 3-word idiom meaning "to start obeying or conforming."

"fall in line" — to start obeying the rules or conforming to expected behavior.
⏸ ~13:00

During a tense first surgery, the other interns place bets on whether their nervous friend will succeed. Listen for a 3-word idiom meaning "to succeed at something difficult."

"pull it off" — to succeed at doing something difficult or risky.
⏸ ~35:15

A senior doctor recognizes a new intern's family resemblance instantly. Listen for a 3-word idiom meaning "to look exactly like someone."

"the spitting image (of someone)" — looking almost exactly identical to another person, usually a family member.

Section 3

Comprehension Check

Answer in your own words
1
What does Dr. Bailey's "five rules" speech tell the new interns about hospital life?
2
Why does George get chosen for the first surgery, and what goes wrong during it?
3
How do Meredith and Cristina figure out what's wrong with their patient, Katie?
4
What does Meredith discover about her mother at the end of the episode?
5
What was the most intense or memorable moment for you in the episode?
