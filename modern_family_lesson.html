Vocabulary

Words from the episode

The extended family gathers for a big wedding day, and almost everything that can happen does. Kids say things at the worst possible moments, parents try to follow old family customs, and the couple getting married just wants the day to go smoothly — with very mixed results.

Read each sentence. Choose the best meaning for the highlighted word.

1. The actors had one more rehearsal before the show opened to the public.

2. There was an awkward silence after he forgot her name in front of everyone.

3. Don't embarrass me by telling that story in front of my boss!

4. She has three siblings: two brothers and one sister.

5. He tends to exaggerate how difficult his job is.

6. Eating a big dinner together on Sundays is a tradition in their family.

7. After her parents remarried, she grew up in a blended family with two stepbrothers.

Section 3

Discussion

Talk about the episode
1
Modern Family shows several very different family structures living in the same extended family. Which family in the show do you relate to most, and why?
2
Kids in the show often say funny or surprising things without realizing it. Do you remember something funny a child once said to you?
3
The show uses a "mockumentary" style, with characters talking directly to the camera. Do you think this makes the comedy funnier? Why or why not?
4
Weddings, parties, and family traditions appear often in the show. What is your favorite family celebration?
5
If you were part of this extended family, which character do you think you would get along with best?
